About US

Tecnoesis, the annual techno-management festival of National Institute of Technology Silchar, is one of the grandest carnivals of North-East India that attracts tech-savvies and geeks with their ingenious innovations from all over the country. Over the years, Tecnoesis keeps getting bigger, better and louder, and thus has been providing a platform to new-fangled ideas and budding brains, aiding them in reaching their full potential. As such, Team Tecnoesis imputes campus ambassadors from different institues all over India, with the objective to publicise Tecnoesis in and out of their college which in turn also boosts their management, leadership and organisation skills.

Why CA ?

Being a campus ambassador provides an opportunity to not only witness the extravagance and grandeur of Tecnoesis but sharpen your communication and technical knacks also, and as such would also make you familiar with the know-how of organising a college-level fest. Forming a network with thousands of brilliant mind from across the nation will always be valuable and this gives you a chance to get that done. This is going to give you a go in imbibing techno-entrepreneurship skillset. Besides, a few incentives have also been enlisted below.

Responsiblities

Coordination

Coordinating with the team of your institute, your institute's administration and Team Tecnoesis which helps in organising various publicity workshops with ease. You are thus the single point of contact between us and your entire institute

College Publicity

College Publicity which includes display of posters on students' notice boards and emailing the various details about Tecnoesis to the concerned participants.

Social Media

Social Media activity regarding Tecnoesis and contributing to reaching out to a wider mass of enthusiasts through various social media platforms.

Incentives

Certificate of appreciation undersigned by Tecnoesis'20 NIT Silchar

Opportunity to win exclusive Tecnoesis'20 goodies and also e-goodies.

Recognition in the form of name inclusion in the official Tecnoesis'20 website and social media handles.

Top campus ambassadors will be felicitated with Special Appreciation Award

Get to become a part of Campus Ambassador diaries through Technoesis Facebook Page

Few Top Ambassadors will be given free passes to Technoesis'20 NIT SILCHAR
