Hydro Rocketry

“Creativity involves breaking out of established patterns in order to look at things in a different way.”

Planeoder

“If you were born without wings, do nothing to prevent them from growing.”
ASME NIT Silchar brings you the event PLANEADOR providing you a platform to showcase your engineering skills, imagination and creativity will be at stake. In this competition, the students are required to make a glider that is supported in flight by the dynamic reaction of the air against its lifting surfaces, and whose free flight does not depend on an engine. There is no restriction in the material you choose to use in order to make the structure of the glider, and the design should as aerodynamic as possible. So do come and participate in this exciting event in order to enhance your knowledge and make sure to have a lot of fun.
