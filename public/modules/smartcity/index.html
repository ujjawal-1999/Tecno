MY CITY MY DREAM

What is MY CITY MY DREAM?

Tecnoesis 2020 presents you the most awaited event “My City My Dream” ,which comes under SMART CITY module.Nowadays everyone is aware of smart city. A smart city is a designation given to a city that incorporates information and communication technologies (ICT) to enhance the quality and performance of urban services such as energy, transportation and utilities in order to reduce resource consumption, wastage and overall costs.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

The smart city aims on implementing your smart city features that affects the urban areas. Under this event the participants have to create a powerpoint presentation based on the following points:

  • What is Smart city ?
  • Aspects that need to be developed under smart city- Smart Irrigation and water management,smart waste management ,smart parking ,traffic control etc.
  • How can you make a city smart?
    -Take any city except SILCHAR for instance and implement the ideas of smart city.
  • Smart SILCHAR!
    -Suggest an idea that you want to implement in SILCHAR to make it smart.

GUIDELNES

  • Participants need to form groups with 2-4 members.
  • All the team members should be college students belonging to the same educational institutions.
  • The powerpoint presentation has to be submitted 3 days before the day of event.
  • Maximum time to present your slide is 5 minutes after which queries will be arised on your ideas.
  • The dress code will be formal.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Evaluation will be done based on your content and it’s presentation.
  • Your communication skills will be considered.
  • Your knowledge on smart city will also be a judging factor.

ARCHINOVA

ABOUT THE EVENT

It's an enchanting event which makes you recall of your school lullaby days. In this event, you need to showcase your instinctive ideas and improvisations that you opt to suggest in the present constructive sites/or to initiate new constructions by preparing a model of it using various equipments(thermocol,match sticks, colours etc.)

Goals

Implicating your innovative ideas in various constructions that you want to see in your dream city.

Motivation

Archinova provides the platform to exhibit your innovations and constructive ideas that are bumping your brain cells. Thereby, if the theme of the model is found to be fruitful, you will get the opportunity to get your idea implemented in the campus as well as you can send your data report to the district authority. The Gymkhana park and the fountain near the gate of NIT Silchar are the implementations of the blooming buds in Archinova. In accordance with, it's a low expense event which can further be forwarded to the event of "My city my dream " of Smart City Module. Exclaiming your ideas to the viewers enhance your communication skills.Hence ,get yourself entangled in this enthusiastic event with a mass participation.

Rules

  • Team may consist of maximum 5 people.
  • Each team should prepare a PowerPoint presentation of the ideas that you have implemented in your model.
  • No registration fee is required.
  • Expense of the model shall be borrowed by the respective team.
  • Team members should explain their theme to the viewers.

JUDGING CRITERIA

It depends on the marking grade of the viewers

  • 50% - By Director, Deans and Faculty.
  • 30% - 4th year btech , Mtech scholars, PhD scholars
  • 10% - 3rd btech students
  • 10% - General viewers

PS - The judgement of the organising team after overall evaluation will be the final one.

CADECORE

What Is CADecore??

It is a platform which will justify your designing skills.It is basically based on designing software named AUTOCad,which is used to create the computer aided designs or software applications including drafting.

RULES

  • There will be two questions that we will provide at the time of the event.
  • You have to bring your laptop with AUTOCad installed in it.
  • Any technical problem in your laptop will be your responsibility.
  • Keep your laptop fully charged and try to bring the mouse with you.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • The one who submitted the drawing first will get more priority.
  • Dimensions should be same as provided in the question.
  • Accuracy of the participant will be considered.
