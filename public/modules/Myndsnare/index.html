ABOUT MyndSnare

The quizzing extravaganza under Tecnoesis, the annual techno-management fest of NIT Silchar, Conceived in 2010, MyndSnare has gain popularity exponentially. Now regarded as one of the finest quizzing events of the region, MyndSnare has previously featured quizmasters of national repute including Late Vikram Joshi, Arnab Basu, Chandrakant Nair, Maharnab Gogoi, Aryapriya Ganguly, Abhra Das, Kapinjal Kumar Sharma, Manimugdha S Sharma, Kushan Patel and many more.
