INFO

Digitization of the immunization process

INFO

Problem Description : Vaccination is very integral to the health of a child. We generally see that people forget to vaccinate their child for various purposes which may include lack of availability of nearby centres, or just doesn't give much importance. There is a need for a platform for both parents as well as the health centres that can help manage this process. The platform should update the parents on the remaining vaccines that need to be given and should also remind the health centres with information with which they can follow up with the child.

NGO Monitoring

INFO

Problem Description : Monitoring activities of various NGO’s so that they can be provided with necessary funding and making legal paper works and permission granting online. There are various NGO operating across the country. There are many NGO’s recognized by the government and funded by them while some are still in need of governmental aid. We want a portal to make funding and permission grant to such organizations easier and faster. Permission grant includes taking permissions to organize awareness rallies, donation camps and various others which needs to be taken from some legal authorities. The portal should also contain scope through which a governmental body can invite applications from such organizations to help in existing centrally funded projects. Also, a system to provide governmental aid(fund) should be created based on how actively the NGO is working.( Create separate sections based on the cause the NGO is supporting.)

Strengthen surveillance using computer vision

INFO

Problem Description : Looking up at all the security issues in our country today, there needs to be an automated surveillance system that can detect anomalies, fight, theft, harassment of any sort etc. and report itself to the nearest police station. How to Reach

01.

AIR

There are daily direct flights to Silchar from cities like Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, Guwahati and Imphal. Air India, Spice Jet and Jet Airways operate flights from Silchar to Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, Guwahati and Imphal. From airport, the Institute campus is approximately 35 Kms. Hired private taxi service is available from the airport. The Training and Placement Cell helps recruiters reach the campus from the Airport.

02.

RAIL

Silchar has a direct rail link with Kolkata, Delhi, Guwahati. The campus is around 10 KMs from the Silchar railway station. The Training and Placement Cell helps recruiters reach the campus from the railway Station.

03.

ROAD

Silchar is well connected with various cities through road. A 326 km long National Highway directly connects Silchar with Guwahati. A large number of day/night bus ply between Guwahati and Silchar in the morning and evening everyday. After you arrive at ISBT-Silchar. The Training and Placement Cell helps recruiters reach the campus.
